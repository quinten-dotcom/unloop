Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Unloop ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use the Unloop app.

What we collect

Unloop stores all data locally on your device only. We do not have servers, user accounts, or any backend that receives your personal data. Specifically:

  • Your daily practice completions, streak count, and Human Score are stored only on your device using local storage.
  • Your Pause schedule and selected apps are stored only on your device.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any personally identifiable information.

Push notifications

If you grant permission, Unloop may send local push notifications to remind you about your daily practices. These notifications are scheduled on-device. We do not use a third-party push service that would transmit your device token to our servers.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not track how you use the app.

Third-party services

Unloop does not share any data with third parties. We do not use advertising networks, social login, or any third-party data processors.

Data deletion

All your data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time by using the "Reset everything" option in Settings, or by uninstalling the app.

Children

Unloop is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the date at the top. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
